Data Management

Load Strfry

Use negentropy to download FOLLOWS, MUTES and REPORTS data (kinds 3, 1984, 10000) from a Nostr relay.

Default filter includes FOLLOWS (kind 3), REPORTS (kind 1984), and MUTES (kind 10000) 

            

            
            

                
Load Neo4j

                
Transfer all available kind 3, 1984 and 10000 data from strfry into Neo4j. This will insert NostrUser nodes connected by FOLLOWS, REPORTS and MUTES relationships. This operation may take 10-15 minutes to complete.

Strfry Router Management

            

                
Stream Filtered Content

                
Update strfry-router.config to stream content in the form of kinds 1, 7, and 30818 events (may add more later).

                
The brainstorm strfry plugin is implemented to this section of this config, which uses the whitelist based on the GrapeRank influence score (assuming it's been calculated -- don't forget to do this!).

                
Unfiltered baseline WoT events (kinds 3, 1984, and 10000) and profile events (kind 0) continue to stream whether or not the plugin is active.
